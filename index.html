Hi! I'm a full-stack web developer specializing in Ruby on Rails and JavaScript. I just completed General Assembly's 12-week Web Development Immersive program. I'm now hungry for the opportunity to work for a passionate, team-oriented tech company in New York as a junior developer.

I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Mathematics in 2006 but spent the last eight years working as a professional modern dancer in New York City, two years of which were with the historic Merce Cunningham Dance Company. Making a website that documented and shared knowledge about the craft of dance was what helped me discover my intense interest in web development and prompted me to make a career transition.

I'm currently enjoying deepening my knowledge of Rails and Javascript while exploring Backbone and honing my coding and problem-solving skills.

Projects

Conway's Game of Life

This simulation of John Conway's math-based, cellular automaton allows the user to create game states, move the game forward or back in time, change grid sizes, change speeds, and load past game states. The most fun part of the project was tackling the feature of detecting oscillations and still-life objects in the game.

Technologies:

  • JavaScript
  • JQuery
  • Ruby on Rails
  • Jasmine

DanceShare

DanceShare allows audience members to write reviews of modern dance concerts. Users can create dance companies which have runs of performances, and other users can then write reviews. DanceShare also functions as an ongoing dance archive.
